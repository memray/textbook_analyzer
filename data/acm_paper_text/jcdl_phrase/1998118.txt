a link-based visual-search engine for wikipedia this paper introduces hmpara , a new search engine that aims to make wikipedia easier to explore . it works on top of the encyclopedia 's existing link-structure , abstracting away from document-content and allowing users to navigate the resource at a higher level . it utilizes semantic-relatedness measures to emphasize articles and connections that are most likely to be of interest , visualization to expose the structure of how the available information is organized , and lightweight information-extraction to explain itself .
